About

Nora graduated from Bates College with a double-degree in Studio Art and Theater Design in May of 2018. She initially pursued a career in costume design in New York City and Washington, DC. Working for companies such as Parsons-Meares, Ltd. on productions like "Frozen: The Broadway Musical" and the National Opera on "Eugene Onegin," Nora decided to move West to Salt Lake City to pursue a better work and life balance.

Web development and design offered Nora an opportunity to explore another creative industry while leading an adventuous lifestyle skiing, climbing, and hiking in the Wasatch. She still designs and fabricates costumes independently on the side.
